Screen

A computer monitor is an electronic device that shows pictures for computers. Monitors often look like smaller televisions.

C.P.U

A CPU, or central processing unit, is the brain of the computer. Like a human brain, the CPU controls all the functions of the computer. The CPU's job is to listen for input data from devices like a mouse or other software.

Keyboard

A keyboard contains many push buttons. Push buttons are called 'Keys. ' It allows a person to enter letters, numbers, and other symbols into a computer. The keyboard is connected to the computer with a wire or USB.

Mouse

A mouse is a small device that a computer user pushes across a desk surface in order to point to a place on a display screen and to select one or more actions to take from that position.

Speaker

Speakers are standard output devices used with computer systems that enable the listener to listen to a sound as an outcome. Some speakers are used once they have been linked to a computer.
